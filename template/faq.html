Frequently Asked Questions

How to discuss my project?

Signup for an account and login to your account.In your dashboard you will find send mail option.Click on it and send message.

How do i order new project?

Login to your dashboard and click on send project button.You will see a form with title and file input option.Give a title in the title box and upload your project details files as txt,rar,zip,pdf,image(jpg,jpeg,png) or mp4 file format and send it to us.After analyzing your project we will get back to you by email.

How do i make deposit?

In dashboard click on add fund and follow the direction.

How much to pay in advance?

You have to pay 50% of the total project cost in advance.

How project will be delivered to me?

After completing the project we will send you an email with the project files.

Which payment methods do you support?

You can pay us via credit card,debit card, mastercard,wire transfer,skrill and neteller

Do you provide refund?

If you are not satisfied with the completed project we will refund you(no question will be asked).

Do you provide fullstack web developmet service?

Yes, we do.
